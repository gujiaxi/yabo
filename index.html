Past Systems
  • Ultra-wideband (2 GHz of bandwith)
  • A large power source
  • a 8-foot long antenna array
Wi-Vi
  • Low-bandwidth (a 20MHz-wide Wi-Fi channel)
  • Compact (a smaller 3-antenna MIMO radio)
  • Low-power
  • Accessible to non-military entities
Difficulties
  • Signal power is reduced rapidly after traversing the wall twice.
  • The reflections from the wall itself are much stronger. (Flash Effect)
